🌎 Who Has Adopted the IHRA Definition?

The IHRA (International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance) established the internationally accepted definition of antisemitism. Countries, cities, and organizations adopt it to identify and prevent antisemitism.

47 countries that adopted the definition
553 entities (cities, universities, organizations)
