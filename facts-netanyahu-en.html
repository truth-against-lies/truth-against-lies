✅ The Achievements — What Strengthened Israel

Security, diplomatic, and economic achievements under Netanyahu's leadership

Iron Dome — The Life-Saving Defense System

Deployed from 2011, 10 batteries, 4,000+ interceptions
Developed under the Olmert government but deployed, expanded and funded under Netanyahu. Interception rate of 90-97%. Protected millions of citizens. The US funded $1.6B+ thanks to cooperation that Netanyahu advanced.
✓ The result:

The world's most effective defense system — 4,000+ successful interceptions. Saved thousands of lives.

Sources: CSIS | IDF Spokesperson | US Congress

David's Sling + Arrow 3 — Layered Air Defense

David's Sling operational from 2017 | Arrow 3 — exo-atmospheric interception
Israel built a multi-layered air defense system unique in the world. David's Sling intercepted ballistic missiles fired from Iran (range ~1,500 km). Arrow 3 intercepts outside the atmosphere. Both systems proved themselves in Iran's attacks (April and October 2024).
✓ The result:

Multi-layered defense that made Israel the world leader in air defense.

Sources: Rafael | IAI | IDF

Iron Beam — World's First Operational Laser

December 2025 — entered operational service
The world's first laser system to enter operational military service. Interception cost: pennies of electricity compared to $10,000-$100,000+ per interceptor missile. Netanyahu announced an investment of 350 billion NIS over the coming decade in the defense industry.
✓ The result:

A revolution in air defense — interception at the cost of pennies instead of tens of thousands of dollars.

Sources: Jerusalem Post | Euronews | Rafael

Abraham Accords — Diplomatic Breakthrough

September 15, 2020 — The White House
Normalization with UAE, Bahrain, Morocco, Sudan — tripled the number of Arab states with formal ties (from 2 to 6). The first since Jordan in 1994 — a 26-year gap. Opened direct flights, intelligence sharing, and trade. Note: Brokered primarily by the Trump team (Kushner).
✓ The result:

4 new peace agreements — a historic diplomatic breakthrough after 26 years of stagnation.

Sources: Wikipedia | AJC | Atlantic Council

US Embassy in Jerusalem + Golan Heights Recognition

May 2018 + March 2019
Trump moved the embassy to Jerusalem — fulfilling a decades-old diplomatic dream. March 2019: The US recognized for the first time Israeli sovereignty over the Golan Heights. Both achievements were a product of the personal relationship between Netanyahu and Trump.
✓ The result:

American recognition of Jerusalem as Israel's capital and sovereignty over the Golan Heights — a historic change.

Sources: NPR | CNBC

"Campaign Between the Wars" — Hundreds of Strikes in Syria

2013–2023 — Hundreds of strikes on Iranian targets
From January 2013, Israel conducted systematic strikes against Iranian weapons shipments, Hezbollah targets, and IRGC infrastructure in Syria. January 2017–September 2018: 202 Iranian targets were bombed with 800+ bombs and missiles — a strike every 3 days on average. The campaign prevented permanent Iranian military entrenchment on Israel's border.
✓ The result:

Prevention of Iranian military entrenchment in Syria — 800+ bombs and missiles on 202 targets.

Sources: Wikipedia | Jerusalem Post | IDF

Exposure of Iran's nuclear program — contributed to the US withdrawal from the nuclear deal.

Sources: Times of Israel | JNS | Haaretz

Elimination of Hezbollah's leadership — Nasrallah eliminated after 32 years. A strategic shift in the region.

Destruction of Iran's nuclear infrastructure — preventing a nuclear holocaust.
